Building The Core Team

We know that building a team is always the most difficult part of any business or project. A doxed team is something on which the future of a project relies entirely. Finding the core team members' was not that easy for us at PXN, although the astonishing vison of our founders made it easy to grab the attention of like minded passionate people who wanted to be a part of this massive project. We started with a group of friends then eventually found our weaknesses and filled those gaps with few potential people from different platforms. Finalising The ARTWORK

Art is the 'heart and soul' of an NFT project. at the beginning we had the vison and some sample collection  but our artwork was not up to the mark, we needed to do something to make our art 10/10. We started our hunt for a passionate artist, after shuffling through and interviewing many artist we finally found the ONE. She was not aware of the NFT meta but was a fantastic artist since her childhood. "Drawing, to her is elixir of life". as i shuffled through her past works i was getting more and more impressed by her art work. She wanted to learn and we wanted a passionate artist.
